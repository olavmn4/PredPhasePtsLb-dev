MCSR Ranked S10 Phase Points Tracker

Each season is split into 4 monthly phases. Your Elo rank at phase end earns Phase Points which accumulate across all phases. MSS tournament points are included in current totals.

P1, P2, and P3 are now locked. We are currently in Phase 4 — the final phase of Season 10.

qualification

Top 12 — auto-qualify for Playoffs bracket
Top 100 — invited to Last-Chance Qualifier (LCQ) for 4 remaining spots

Ties in Phase Points are broken by final season Elo. All players tied at the 100th-place predicted PP value also qualify for LCQ.

"to qualify" column

Shows how many Elo points a player needs to gain to reach the next milestone:

· Outside top 100 — Elo needed to reach LCQ qualification
· Rank 13–100 — Elo needed to reach Playoffs (top 12)
· Rank 2–12 — Elo needed to move up one seed (beat the player above)
· Rank 1 — nothing to gain

The gap is calculated using the Phase 4 points table: we find the lowest Elo rank at which currentPP + phase4Points[rank] meets the target, then show the Elo difference to the player currently at that rank.
